Research Intern

Jul 2017 - Apr 2018

Intel Labs

Path-finding is an important topic in applications such as autonomous driving, robots and drones in order to navigate cluttered environments. I developed a new approach that operates directly on the grid or bitmap rather than first converting it to a graph making the implementation very hardware friendly. I applied a hierarchical approach that reduce the search space, identifying the areas of interest in high levels of abstraction, allowing the tree to be pruned as it is constructed, using an heuristic based on density and distance the algorithm gives priority to the shortest paths in areas with less obstacles.
